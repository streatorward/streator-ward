About me

I'm a web developer with a strong interest in projects that require both conceptual and analytical thinking. I'm fully-committed to designing and developing innovative web-based materials that users will love. I'm always eager to learn more tricks from anyone - regardless of the industry they're working in. I became interested in technology at a young age and my grandfather introduced me to video games and the hardware side of things. As I got older my mind continued to grow along with my passion and curiosity for learning all that I could about the world of technology. This curiosity lead me to NSS so I could begin to fully understand and learn what really goes in to making the technology I grew to love actually work.
